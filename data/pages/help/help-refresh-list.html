Manually refresh your filters

uBlock Origin will automatically refresh your filter list once a day on all your browsers. For recent modifications to be applied, you should trigger a manual refresh.

  • Open the uBlock Origin dashboard (see above)
  • Click on the Filters lists tab, scroll to the bottom
  • Locate the "letsblock.it - My filters" list at the bottom
  • Click on the clock icon next to it, then on Update now
